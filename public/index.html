How I Develop

"There are no secrets to success. It is the result of preparation, hard work, and learning from failure." - Colin Powell

Design

A great app starts with great design. I take the time to design before coding so that my vision can be executed flawlessly.

Learn

Learning never stops and nowhere is that more true than in development. SDG gave me a passion to keep learning after the classroom.

Communicate

Client or personal communication is key. A great app can be derailed by poor communication, so I always keep my ears open.

Develop

Develop with intent. A great app ultimately comes down to putting in the work by writing clean code and developing with intent.

Deliver Results

On time and right the first time. My apps are completed on time and correctly. A great app is nothing unless you deliver on your promise.

Evolve

Done coding? You're not done. Development is constantly evolving and my passion for coding will ensure I evolve with it.
